Privacy Policy

For Unit Converter + Math Tools (Chrome Extension) by Eagleye Web Solutions No data sold No tracking

Effective Date

December 31, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains what information the extension collects and how it is used.

Summary

  • We do not collect personal information.
  • We do not sell any data.
  • We do not use analytics, ads, or tracking.
  • Any saved settings (like theme, decimals, favorites, history) are stored using chrome.storage and remain in your browser.
  • The extension works offline and does not require a server.

Information We Collect

None.

The extension does not collect, transmit, or store personal data on external servers.

Data Stored Locally in Your Browser

To provide features like favorites, history, theme settings, and decimal precision, the extension stores the following data locally using Chrome’s extension storage:

  • Theme preference (Light/Dark/System)
  • Decimal precision setting
  • Favorited conversions (category + from/to units)
  • Recent conversion history (last results)
  • Last selected converter category and units

This information is used only to improve your experience and is not shared with Eagleye Web Solutions or any third party.

Permissions

The extension requests the following permission:

  • storage — used to save your settings, favorites, and history in Chrome.

No other permissions are required for the extension to function.

Third-Party Services

None.

The extension does not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, trackers, or external APIs.

Children’s Privacy

This extension is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this Privacy Policy, we will revise the “Effective Date” at the top of this page.
