Converter Suite – Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 31, 2025

Converter Suite (Unit Converter + Math Tools) is developed and maintained by Eagleye Web Solutions. We are committed to protecting your privacy and keeping your data fully under your control.

Data Collection

Converter Suite does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any user data. Eagleye Web Solutions has no access to your information.

Local Data Storage

Converter Suite stores limited data locally on your device using browser or app storage solely to provide core functionality:

  • Unit converter preferences (category, from/to units)
  • Favorite conversions
  • Recent conversion history
  • Theme preference (light / dark / system)
  • Decimal precision settings

This data never leaves your device and is never accessible to Eagleye Web Solutions.

Network & Internet Access

Converter Suite operates fully offline. It does not communicate with external servers and does not require an internet connection to function.

Third-Party Services

Converter Suite does not use analytics tools, advertising networks, tracking scripts, or third-party SDKs.

What We Do Not Collect

  • Personally identifiable information
  • Browsing history or website content
  • Location data
  • Authentication or financial data
  • Analytics, advertising, or tracking data

Children’s Privacy

Converter Suite is not directed toward children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes, the “Last updated” date above will be revised accordingly.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you may contact Eagleye Web Solutions through the Chrome Web Store listing or the official project website.
