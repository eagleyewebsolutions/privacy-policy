Toolbox Hub – Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 29, 2025

Toolbox Hub is developed and maintained by Eagleye Web Solutions. We are committed to protecting your privacy and keeping your data fully under your control.

Data Collection

Toolbox Hub does not collect, transmit, sell, or share user data. Eagleye Web Solutions has no access to your information.

Local Data Storage

All data created within Toolbox Hub is stored locally on your device using Chrome’s extension storage:

  • Todos and task metadata
  • Notes and note templates
  • Weather preferences (favorites and units)
  • Vault entries (encrypted locally)

This data never leaves your device.

Location Access

Toolbox Hub may request access to your location only when you explicitly click “Use my location” in the Weather section.

  • Location is used only to fetch local weather information
  • Location data is not stored
  • Location data is not shared or tracked

Third-Party Services

Toolbox Hub uses public weather APIs solely to retrieve weather data requested by the user. No personal information is sent beyond what is required to fulfill the request.

What We Do Not Collect

  • Personally identifiable information
  • Browsing history or website content
  • Authentication or financial data
  • Analytics, advertising, or tracking data

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you may contact Eagleye Web Solutions through the Chrome Web Store listing.
