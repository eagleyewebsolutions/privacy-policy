Arcade Deck Privacy Policy

Arcade Deck is a local-first arcade games extension. Your privacy is a core feature.

Effective date: 2025-12-31

Summary

  • No accounts
  • No tracking
  • No analytics
  • No ads
  • No cloud sync
  • All data stays on your device

What data Arcade Deck stores

Arcade Deck stores basic game data such as scores, best scores, last scores, and preferences (for example light/dark theme) locally on your device using browser storage (localStorage).

What data Arcade Deck does NOT collect

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number, address)
  • Browsing history
  • Location data
  • Health or financial information
  • Any analytics or behavioral tracking
  • Any data sent to external servers

Data sharing

Arcade Deck does not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties. There are no external servers involved.

Reset / Clear Data

Arcade Deck includes a “Reset” option to clear saved stats and preferences. You can also clear extension site data through your browser settings at any time.

Permissions

Arcade Deck uses storage only to save and load your data locally. It does not access your browsing history or transmit data.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you may contact Eagleye Web Solutions through the Chrome Web Store listing.
