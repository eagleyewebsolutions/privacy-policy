FocusPad Privacy Policy

FocusPad is a local-first Todo & Notes extension. Your privacy is a core feature.

Effective date: 2025-12-29

Summary

  • No accounts
  • No tracking
  • No analytics
  • No cloud sync
  • All data stays on your device

What data FocusPad stores

FocusPad stores your todos, notes, and settings (such as Focus Mode and last opened view) using Chrome’s local storage (chrome.storage.local) on your device.

What data FocusPad does NOT collect

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number, address)
  • Browsing history
  • Location data
  • Health or financial information
  • Any analytics or behavioral tracking

Data sharing

FocusPad does not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties. There are no external servers involved.

Export / Import

If you use Export, FocusPad creates a backup file on your device that you control. Import restores data from a file you choose.

Permissions

FocusPad uses the storage permission only to save and load your data locally.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you may contact Eagleye Web Solutions through the Chrome Web Store listing.
